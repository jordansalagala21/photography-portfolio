About Me

Hi, I'm Ava.

I'm a third-year photography student focused on portrait and documentary work. My practice centers on natural light, honest expressions, and the in-between moments that usually go unnoticed. I'm currently building my technical range across film and digital while developing a personal visual language rooted in stillness and quiet observation.

When I'm not shooting, I'm assisting on local shoots, printing in the darkroom, or scouting new locations around the city.
